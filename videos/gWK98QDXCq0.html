Effects: glow styles, drop shadows, brightness/contrast, sharpness, rounded corners (fully customizable). Scenes: drag/drop to create, duplicate, reorder; animations between scenes based on matching element names. Hotkeys and Stream Deck integration available. Audio mixer supports multiple sources (microphone, music, app audio) with independent volume control and muting.
