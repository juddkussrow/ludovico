Connectivity options: gigabit Ethernet (40 Mbps streaming), USB high-speed, Wi-Fi. Key applications shown: (1) RF Vision—radio camera mode detecting time-of-arrival differences across antennas to plot signal direction with color-coded frequency; (2) camera decoder—decoding NTSC wireless video signals; (3) augmented reality overlay—combining live phone camera with RF direction-of-arrival data via web interface (quadraf/ar); (4) drone tracking—real-time signal tracking at 30 frames per second across C-band, tracking through foliage and obstacles. Optional Moon RF phased array antenna (240 elements, open-source design) enables Earth-Moon-Earth communication. Pogo pin connectors for tile stacking, 12V power distribution, FFC daisy-chaining for synchronization and data flow across multiple tiles. All drivers and software open-source.
