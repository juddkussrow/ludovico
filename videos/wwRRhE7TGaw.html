Palmier: Open Source AI Video Editor for Mac [2026]

@octopusinc_ · 2026-06-28 · Video Gen

Palmier Pro is an open-source, Swift-native video editor for macOS that integrates AI video generation directly into a multi-track timeline. It allows users to generate, edit, and export AI-created video clips alongside traditional footage, supporting models like Kling, Seedance, and Veo. While the editor itself is free and open-source, AI generation requires paid credits, and it is currently exclusive to Apple Silicon Macs running macOS 26 Tahoe.
